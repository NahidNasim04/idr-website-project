About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on preparing professionals for the evolving world of digital, cyber and technology risk.

Through collaboration with academic institutions and industry partners, IDR combines research, academic insight and real-world projects to build the next generation of digital risk practitioners.

Our Model

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps designed for students and professionals to build expertise in digital and cyber risk.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing future risk models, governance frameworks and AI risk management solutions.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement digital risk frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2.

Our Pipeline

Community

IDR supports a growing community of students, early-career professionals and practitioners working in sectors such as financial services, technology and critical infrastructure seeking to upskill in digital and cyber risk.
